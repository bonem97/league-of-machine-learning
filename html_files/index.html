Machine Learning Project: League of Legends Prediction Models

Which team is going to win based on data and statistics 10 minutes into the match?

In this project, machine learning is being utilized to predict the winner of a League of Legends match based on the events/actions that have led up to the 10 minute mark.

League of Legends is a 5v5 online game consisting of 3 lanes, 11 towers, a jungle with side objectives, and a nexus, which is the main objective of the game.
